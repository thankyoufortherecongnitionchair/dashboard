Welcome to Project Athena

Project Athena is an NGO dedicated to breaking the taboos surrounding menstrual hygiene in India and beyond.

Our Global Mission:

At Project Athena, we envision a world where menstruation is celebrated as a natural and essential aspect of life. We are committed to extending our outreach to lesser privileged countries, striving to create a positive and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of their socio-economic background.

Beyond providing menstrual hygiene products, we focus on comprehensive education and awareness programs. We believe that knowledge is a powerful tool for change, and through our initiatives, we aim to empower communities to embrace menstrual health with dignity and pride.

Project Athena operates on the principles of inclusivity, equality, and sustainability. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to implement sustainable solutions that not only address immediate needs but also contribute to the long-term well-being of communities. Together, we can break the silence and eliminate the barriers that hinder menstrual hygiene access globally.

Join us in this transformative journey as we advocate for a world where menstruation is understood, respected, and celebrated by all. Together, we can make a lasting impact and create positive change for generations to come.
